Nextel eSIM Activation Steps

Follow these steps to activate your Nextel Diamond or Royal eSIM and begin earning from calls, SMS, WebGain, and referrals every day.

1

Open the official Nextel website

Use the official domain nextelwebsite.com and avoid links from unknown sources. This ensures you're following the correct activation process and payment instructions.

2

Read the Nextel eSIM plans

Review the Diamond eSIM (₦10,500 — local roaming, WebGain ₦7–₦20/visit) and Royal eSIM (₦17,500 — global roaming, calls reward ₦288–₦628/min, WebGain ₦15–₦50/visit). Choose based on your goals.

3

Follow official payment instructions

Use only the payment details and instructions shown on the official Nextel plans page. Save your payment screenshot or receipt — it will be required during the activation process.

4

Receive your connect bonus

Once activated, Diamond subscribers receive ₦8,000 and Royal subscribers receive ₦14,000 as a connect bonus — credited instantly to your Nextel Cloud dashboard.

5

Set up your Nextel Cloud and start earning

Log in to your Nextel Cloud dashboard, activate your personal website (WebGain), add contacts to your virtual phonebook, and begin earning from every call, SMS, website visit, and referral — every single day.

How do I activate a Nextel eSIM?

Go to nextelwebsite.com, choose Diamond (₦10,500) or Royal (₦17,500) eSIM, follow the official payment instructions on the plans page, keep your payment proof, and complete the activation steps provided.

What is the official Nextel activation link?

The only official domain is nextelwebsite.com. The plans page is at nextelwebsite.com/#plans and this activation guide is at nextelwebsite.com/how-to-activate.html. Do not use any other link.

Which plan should I choose: Diamond or Royal?

Choose Diamond (₦10,500) for local roaming and standard earning rates. Choose Royal (₦17,500) for global roaming, higher calls reward (up to ₦628/min), higher WebGain (up to ₦50/visit), saved contacts earnings, and 1% turnover commission.
