Nextel Cloud — FAQ

What is Nextel Cloud? +
Nextel Cloud is the official Nextel subscriber dashboard where you track your daily earnings from calls, SMS, WebGain website traffic, saved contacts, referrals, and 1% daily turnover commission — all in one place.
How do I access Nextel Cloud? +
Nextel Cloud access is provided after your eSIM account is activated. Register for Diamond or Royal eSIM on nextelwebsite.com, complete activation via the official Telegram channel, and receive your Cloud dashboard login credentials.
Is Nextel Cloud on mobile? +
Yes. Nextel Cloud is fully mobile-optimized and works on any Android, iOS, or desktop browser. No app download required — access it directly from your phone.
Does Nextel Cloud show WebGain earnings? +
Yes. Nextel Cloud tracks every visit to your personal Nextel website and updates your WebGain balance in real time. Diamond plan earns ₦7–₦20 per visit; Royal plan earns ₦15–₦50 per visit.
How do I withdraw from Nextel Cloud? +
Earnings are withdrawn via the Nextel V-Card, a Verve-powered card that lets you spend anywhere in the world. Request withdrawal directly from your Nextel Cloud dashboard or contact the official Telegram channel for assistance.
