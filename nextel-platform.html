What Nextel is

What is the Nextel Connect platform?

Nextel Connect is Nigeria's NCC-licensed, CAC-registered virtual telecom service provider. Subscribers activate a Diamond or Royal eSIM and earn daily rewards from calls, SMS, personal website traffic, saved contacts, referrals, and network turnover.

01

Nextel eSIM

An electronic SIM you activate on your phone that earns you money every day — from calls made, SMS sent, your personal Nextel website, and more.

02

Nextel Cloud Dashboard

Your personal control panel where you track total earnings, virtual line status, phonebook call rewards, and referral commissions in real time.

03

Nextel V-Card

A Verve-powered card that lets you spend your Nextel earnings anywhere in the world — including online purchases and POS transactions.

04

WebGain — Your Personal Website

Every subscriber gets a personal Nextel website that earns ad revenue every time someone visits. Diamond earns ₦7–₦20 per visit, Royal earns ₦15–₦50.

05

Referral & Turnover Commission

Refer people and earn bonuses. Diamond subscribers earn 0.4% turnover commission; Royal subscribers earn 1% — paid daily from the network's generated revenue.

06

Free Calls, Airtime & Data

Enjoy free local calls, free airtime, and free data alongside your daily earnings — all included in your Nextel eSIM subscription.

FAQ

Nextel platform FAQs

Short answers to the most common questions people ask before activating a Nextel eSIM.

What is the Nextel Connect platform?

Nextel Connect is Nigeria's NCC-licensed, CAC-registered (BN No. 9628017) virtual telecom. Subscribers earn daily from calls, SMS, personal website traffic (WebGain), saved contacts, referrals, and turnover commission — all withdrawable with no minimum.

Is Nextel legit?

Yes. Nextel Network is registered with the CAC under BN No. 9628017 and complies with NCC requirements. Always confirm you are on nextelwebsite.com, use official support channels, and keep payment proof for all transactions.

What is the difference between Diamond and Royal eSIM?

Diamond eSIM costs ₦10,500, comes with ₦8,000 connect bonus, local roaming, SMS reward ₦6–₦20, WebGain ₦7–₦20/visit, and 0.4% turnover commission. Royal eSIM costs ₦17,500, comes with ₦14,000 connect bonus, global roaming, calls reward ₦288–₦628/min, SMS reward ₦13–₦83, WebGain ₦15–₦50/visit, saved contacts ₦72 each, and 1% turnover commission.

What is the Nextel CAC registration number?

Nextel Network's Corporate Affairs Commission registration number is Business Name No. 9628017. You can independently verify this on the CAC public search portal or by contacting Info@nextelconnect.

How do I contact Nextel officially?
