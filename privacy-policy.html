Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026

1. Who We Are

Nextel Network (trading as "Nextel Connect") is a Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) registered business (BN No. 9628017) operating a regulated virtual telecom eSIM service in Nigeria. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit nextelwebsite.com, activate a Nextel eSIM, or use the Nextel Cloud dashboard, and how that information is used and protected.

2. Information We Collect

To activate an eSIM and pay out earnings, we collect information such as your full name, phone number, email address, government-issued identification for verification, bank account or card details for withdrawals, and device information needed to provision your eSIM profile. When you use your personal Nextel website page, we also collect basic analytics such as visit counts, ad impressions, and click activity tied to your WebGain earnings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to activate and manage your eSIM, calculate and credit your Calls Reward, SMS Reward, WebGain, Referral Connect Bonus, Saved Contacts, and Turnover Commission earnings, process withdrawals to your bank account, Nextel V-Card, or as airtime/data, verify your identity for free POS terminal eligibility, communicate service updates, and comply with NCC and CAC regulatory obligations.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share necessary data with regulated partners that make Nextel possible, including telecom infrastructure partners (MTN, Glo, Airtel, 9Mobile), payment processors for withdrawals and the Nextel V-Card (Verve), and advertising partners (such as Google Ads) that power WebGain earnings on your personal website page. We may also disclose information where required by Nigerian law or a valid request from the NCC, CAC, or law enforcement.

5. Data Security

We apply reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your data, including restricted access to subscriber records and encrypted handling of payment information. No system is completely secure, and you should never share your Nextel Cloud login credentials, OTPs, or eSIM activation codes with anyone, including people claiming to represent Nextel.

6. Your Choices and Rights

You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you, ask us to correct inaccurate information, or request closure of your Nextel account and deletion of your data, subject to our regulatory record-keeping obligations. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

7. Cookies and Analytics

nextelwebsite.com and your personal Nextel page use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, measure WebGain ad performance, and improve site reliability. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Nextel eSIM activation requires valid identification and is intended for users who meet the minimum age for telecom subscription and financial transactions under Nigerian law. We do not knowingly collect data from children below this age.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our services evolve or regulations change. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact Us

For privacy questions or data requests, email Info@nextelconnect or reach us on Telegram at @nextelofficial.
